ABOUT US

Rajasthan Restaurant

The Rajasthan Restaurant seeks to redefine the common Indian dining experience, and elevate the cuisine to a status not usually enjoyed and achieved by other curry houses. Diners at Rajasthan enjoy culinary taste of Mogul India, not something other curry houses can match.

The menu comprises of Indian food as it should be, with robust aromas, glowing colours, earthy flavours and the evocative taste of exotic masalas. Diners at Rajasthan enjoy an amalgam of exciting freshly ground spices specially blended uniquely for each individual dish by its devoted chefs. All dishes are cooked to authentic recipes; whilst subtle regional dishes, especially recipes based on rich The Rajasthan specialties not found in other curry houses is enjoyed. Fusion food is becoming increasingly interesting as it is popular, Rajasthan will accommodate the popular palette with its own Rajasthanian interpretation, as well as providing dishes for the culinary purist.
